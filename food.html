5 New Restaurants to Look Out for in 2017

Ever feel hungry, but you don’t know what to eat? You want to try something new, but you’re not sure where to go? We have created a list for the top 5 restaurants to look out for in 2017.

  1. KURO Restaurant & Bar
    The first on our list, is definitely KURO. Although a newly opened restaurant, KURO always seems to be packed with people. Whenever you’re craving Japanese food, come to KURO and try their specialized drinks, their rice bowls, or their udon. Not to mention, their interior provides a great ambiance for you and your friends to socialize in.
  2. Lazeez Shawarma
    Shawarma is definitely a student’s best friend. If you come to Waterloo, then you have definitely seen students lining outside of shawarma shops. Just last year, Lazeez, Mississauga's best shawarma shop has opened a franchise here in the heart of Waterloo! This year, Lazeez has just opened their second shop, closer to the University of Waterloo. If you want something easy to eat, warm, and you love large portion sizes, then go to Lazeez Shawarma!
  3. Hot Star
    In 2015, Hot Star has opened their first restaurant in Canada. Luckily, this franchise has opened another one of its kind in the plaza at King and University Avenue. Hot Star is a Taiwanese restaurant that specializes in huge slices of juicy, fried chicken. You’ll be able to choose from a variety of flavours such as seaweed, salt and pepper, barbeque, and more. We definitely recommend trying the experience of eating chicken the size of your head at least once in your lifetime!
  4. Kono Pizza
    Kono Pizza has just recently opened in a little stand at 203 Lester Street within the past month. If you love eating food in cones, and you love pizza, then you’ll definitely love Kono Pizza! They serve you pizza toppings inside a cone. Forget the mess that you receive when digging into a huge slice of pizza. This pizza is completely versatile for you to bring to class and munch on!
  5. Waterloo Empire Restaurant
    Although Waterloo Empire Restaurant is a previously existing restaurant, the restaurant now has reopened with new management to properly serve all their customers. If you love spicy Chinese food then you should definitely pay a visit to Waterloo Empire Restaurant to try their meals for a great value.
